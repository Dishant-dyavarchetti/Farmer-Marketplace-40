About Us

Welcome to MADHYAMUKT, a dedicated platform that connects farmers directly with buyers. We believe in fair trade, transparency, and sustainability, ensuring that fresh farm produce reaches the right hands at fair prices.

Our goal is to create an efficient and transparent marketplace that benefits both farmers and consumers. We aim to:

  • Empower farmers by providing a direct-to-consumer platform.
  • Ensure fair pricing and eliminate unnecessary middlemen.
  • Promote organic and sustainable farming practices.
  • Offer consumers fresh, locally sourced farm produce.
